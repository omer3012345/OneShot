About OneShot

League of Legends is a multiplayer team-oriented video game based around characters battling against each other to claim victory.

Within the game exists various roles, each with their own responsibilities. In League of Legends, there exists five main roles for the various characters within the video game.

OneShot gives you information about the character name, the character win rate, the character guide, and the character ranking.

OneShot gives you insight on the various champions in the game and helps you pick the best character to win your next game!
