Top Lane Guide

The lone wolf ! The toplaners role is to compete in a 1v1 duel, apply pressure, and push the enemy tower down. Split-pushing is also a key role for toplaners. Notable playstyles of toplaners include:

  • Tanks
    1. Sion
    2. Malphite
    3. Ornn
  • Duelists
    1. Fiora
    2. Jax
  • Ranged
    1. Teemo
    2. Quinn
